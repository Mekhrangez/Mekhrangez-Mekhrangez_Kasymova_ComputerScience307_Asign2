Bird Milk Cake

Ingredients:

Korzh:

  • 4 yolks
  • 70 gr sugar
  • 70 gr butter at room temperature
  • 100 gr flour
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder

Soufflé:

  • 4 squirrels
  • 100 g butter at room temperature
  • 50 gr condensed milk
  • 270 gr sugar
  • 100 ml water
  • 7 gr agar

Ganache:

  • 70 gr dark chocolate
  • 70 gr of heavy cream

Direction:

  1. Let's prepare the dough. Separate the whites from the yolks of eggs. Squirrels are sent to the refrigerator. Add sugar to the yolks and beat in a mixer at high speed until white, while beating, add soft butter. In a bowl, measure out white flour, add baking powder, mix. Pour the resulting mixture into beaten yolks and knead the dough by hand. We cover the bottom of a detachable form with a diameter of 20 centimeters with baking paper and put the dough into it, slightly level it from the middle to the edges. We bake at a temperature of 375F for 17 - 20 minutes. Pour agar with half a glass of water at room temperature and leave to swell. Put the baked cake on a wire rack and peel off the paper.
  2. While the crust is cooling, prepare the soufflé. First, prepare the buttercream. We put soft butter in a mixer bowl and beat it at high speed until white, without stopping beating, gradually add condensed milk at room temperature. From the resulting cream set aside 2 tbsp for decoration. We do not put the finished cream in the refrigerator, leave it at room temperature. Soaked swollen gelatin is transferred to a small saucepan, add sugar and put the saucepan on fire. Constantly stirring, heat up to a temperature of 60 degrees Celsius, turn off and leave to cool. We take out eggs white from the refrigerator, put them in a mixer bowl and beat in a mixer at high speed into a strong foam, without stopping beating, add ¼ teaspoon of citric acid. Continuing to beat, pour in a slightly cooled agar with sugar in a thin stream. Reduce the speed to medium and gradually add the prepared buttercream. With a long, sharp knife, cut the cake in half lengthwise. We put one part on the bottom of a detachable form with a diameter of 20 centimeters and spread half of the cooked soufflé on top, cover with the second half of the cake and lay out the remaining soufflé, level the top. We send the cake to the refrigerator for 1.5 - 2 hours. After 1.5 hours, prepare chocolate icing. Pour 180 grams of cream into a saucepan, add 30 grams of sugar and put on fire. Heat until sugar is completely dissolved. With a sharp knife, carefully separate the cake from the walls of the mold and put it on a large plate or board. Pour the icing that has cooled down to approximately 30 degrees Celsius and level the top, driving the excess onto the sides of the cake. We shift the cake to a large plate or dish on which we will serve it. We send the cake for at least one hour in the refrigerator so that the icing freezes.
