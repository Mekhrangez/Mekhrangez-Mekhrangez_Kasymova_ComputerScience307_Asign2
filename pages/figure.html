Cake Figure

Figure or shape depends on your choice
Ingredients:

Cakes

  • 1 egg
  • 80 gr sugar
  • 40 gr honey
  • a pinch of salt
  • 50 gr butter
  • 0.5 tsp soda
  • 190-200 gr flour

Cream:

  • 200 gr white chocolate
  • 200 gr cream
  • 300 gr cream cheese

Raspberry confiture:

  • 100 gr raspberry puree
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

Direction:

  1. We start with the preparation of honey cakes. You will need: sugar, honey, butter, egg, soda, flour and vinegar. Any honey will do (you can also candied). Mix dry ingredients together: flour and baking soda. In a saucepan combine honey, sugar and butter. Heat until sugar dissolves. A mixture of butter, sugar and honey is hot. If you immediately add the egg, there is a risk that it will curdle. Therefore, first add 1/3 of the flour. You can mix with a mixer or manually with a spatula. Now add egg and flour. Finally, knead the dough. It will be warm and sticky. This is fine. Once the dough has cooled, it will become firmer, less sticky. Therefore, we roll it into a ball and put it in the refrigerator. The dough can be prepared ahead of time. Keeps in the fridge for 2 weeks and in the freezer for a couple of months.
  2. Once the dough has cooled, roll it out to a thickness of 2-3 mm. We do this on the surface where we will bake: silicone mat, parchment paper. Dust the surface with flour to keep the dough from sticking. As part of the dough, soda, which will react during baking, the cake will become thicker. So roll thinner.
  3. I cut out the blanks (stencil) with a knife, removed the rest of the dough. With a fork, make slits on the surface of the dough so that the cake rises evenly and there are no bubbles. Bake the cakes for 8-10 minutes until golden brown at 375F. After baking, the cakes will be soft and pliable, as they cool they will become harder, but still remain soft.
  4. Please note that after cooling, the cakes will become crispy and brittle. This is fine. Additionally, soaking the cakes with syrup / juice and anything else is not required. Moisture from the cream is enough to soak the cakes. They will become soft.
  5. Cream: Melt the heavy wipping cream with chocolate in the microwave in pulses. Then mix everything well and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Then beat it with cream cheese.
  6. Cake assembly: I put the cake on the substrate. The cake on the reverse side can be smeared with a drop of cream, so it will stick to the substrate and will not slip. On the outer and inner perimeters we make a pattern of cream droplets. Try to keep everything the same size. Lay 2 cakes on top and press lightly. Also superimposed droplets and decorate with berries.
