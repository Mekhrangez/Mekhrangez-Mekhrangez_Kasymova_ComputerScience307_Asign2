Macaroons

Ingredients:

  • 83 gr almond flour
  • 83 gr powdered sugar
  • 58 grams of eggs white
  • 58 grams of sugar
  • dyes or cocoa powder
  • espresso - 0.5 tsp

Coffee Cream:

  • mascarpone - 100 g
  • bitter chocolate - 50 g
  • espresso - 1 tbsp

Direction:

Sift powdered sugar, cocoa powder or dyes, plain and almond flour 3 times. Beat egg whites and powdered sugar into strong peaks. Continuing to beat the whites, gradually introduce the dry base along with espresso and knead pasta. Fill a piping bag with pasta and pipe beautiful even halves onto a baking sheet with parchment paper. Leave the blanks at room temperature for half an hour. Preheat oven to 145°C and bake pasta for 10-12 minutes. Melt chocolate in a water bath, pour in espresso, cool and beat with mascarpone. Fill a clean piping bag with the coffee ganache and seal the halves together. Put the pasta cakes in the refrigerator for a day.
