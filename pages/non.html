Oriental Flatbread

Ingredients:

  • 600gr flour
  • 150ml hot water
  • 150ml milk
  • 80ml vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Direction:

Mix flour with salt and dry yeast. Pour in water at room temperature. Knead the dough, let it rise twice, punch down both times and knock it on the table several times. Then divide the dough into 4 parts, form cakes 1.5-2 cm thick, push the middle of the cake with a check or something similar to a check. Lubricate with water, sprinkle with sesame seeds and let stand for 15 minutes. Bake in a preheated oven at 425F for about 12-15 minutes.
