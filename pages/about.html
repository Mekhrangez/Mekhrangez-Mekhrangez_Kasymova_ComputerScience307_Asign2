Bake with Miki

Hello, my name is MiKi, full name is Mehrangez Kasymova. I am from Tajikistan, Central Asia. I enjoy baking, cooking and decorating cakes. I first started baking at the age of 10. Unfortunately, I did not have the opportunity to bake and decorate cakes, only at the age of 18 I was able to fully bake and decorate cakes. In my home country I studied at a medical university and worked in a bakery on vacation, it was a great experience for me to learn more. I also sold my cakes. All this helped me achieve a good result and continue to bake with new desires and strength.

I really hope that you find my site useful and use it. Here you can find such cakes as Berry, Napoleon, Honey, Prague, Bird's milk, Three chocolates, Cookies, Cream for decorations, Mastic and much more.
